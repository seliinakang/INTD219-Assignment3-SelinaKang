SOU FUJIMOTO ARCHITECTS

BETON HALA WATERFRONT CENTRE

Belgrade, Republic of Serbia

2012

The new, vibrant pedestrian square will serve as the principle access point from the capital's riverfront to it's historic core. It will contain retail space, cafes and restaurants, exterior exhibition space and a viewing platform, as it is perched atop a subterranean parking garage and transportation hub linking the ferry terminal, tram and bus.
