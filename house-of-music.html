SOU FUJIMOTO

HOUSE OF MUSIC

Budapest, Hungary

2021

The House of Music will provide its guests a singular artistic experience that combines architecture, landscape, and exhibition design to provide them fresh insights into the creation of music and how it affects our lives. The ambitious architectural project, headed by renowned Japanese designer Sou Fujimoto Architects, is a part of the Liget Budapest Project, Europe's largest cultural development, which aims to create and renovate several cultural institutions, as well as create new recreational opportunities and green spaces within Budapest's central City Park. The project will change the city's cultural experience. The initiative aims to enhance the musical experience by utilizing the interplay of light, sound, and nature.
