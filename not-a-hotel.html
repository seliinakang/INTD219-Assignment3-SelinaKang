SOU FUJIMOTO

NOT A HOTEL ISHIGAKI EARTH

Ishigaki Island, Japan

2023

Not A Hotel Ishigaki was created to accommodate up to 10 guests while remaining "connected to the earth" and is situated on the picturesque island of Ishigaki, which is part of Okinawa in southwest Japan. The structure of the rental hotel villa will resemble a disc with a sloping green roof on top. Large circular holes that will encircle a tree and pond at the lowest part of the roof, surrounded by a walkable meadow-lined roof, are depicted in the hotel-cum-villa's renderings. Since it was not intended to have a front or rear, the layout of the house will provide for views of the surrounding area.
