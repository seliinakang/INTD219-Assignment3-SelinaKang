SOU FUJIMOTO ARCHITECTS

SQUARE AT THE UNIVERSITY OF ST.GALLEN

St.Gallen, Switzerland

2022

SQUARE is made up of open-concept spaces with fifteen rooms spread across three stories that are intended to inspire travelers as they pass from lively squares and lounges to more sedate nooks. Extreme flexibility in the layout of all areas has allowed for the free adjustment of all rooms and open areas. Because of this, the majority of spaces include movable furniture and equipment that precisely suit the needs of the event being held and the number of attendees.
