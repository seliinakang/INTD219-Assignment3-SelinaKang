SOU FUJIMOTO ARCHITECTS

'FOREST OF LIGHT' FOR SALONE DEL MOBILE

Milan, Italy

2016

Taking inspiration from the COS Spring Summer 2016 collection, 'Forest of Light' explores ideas of interaction and perspective; the darkened space is illuminated by towering cones of light that are designed to respond to visitors' movements. Specially-composed sounds, subtle fog and mirrored walls create an immersive, infinite landscape where the conical spotlights become abstracted trees in a shifting forest of light. Built in the 1930s by Italian architect Mario Cereghini, the installation is located in Cinema Arti in Milan's San Babila district. A former theatre, the now derelict venue has inspired the key aspects of Sou Fujimoto's design and provides an expansive backdrop to the installation.
