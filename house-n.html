SOU FUJIMOTO ARCHITECTS

HOUSE N

Oita, Japan

2008

This is a two-person plus a dog home. Three progressively sized shells are nestled inside one another to form the actual house. The entire area is covered by the outermost shell, which makes the garden somewhat indoors. Within the covered outdoor space, a small area is enclosed by a second shell. A third shell reduces the amount of inner space. Residents build their life inside this gradation of domain.
