SOU FUJIMOTO

HOUSE NA

Tokyo, Japan

2011

A house located in the heart of Tokyo's residential area. Living in a house amidst the densely populated neighborhood of modest homes and buildings is like living within a tree. A tree has several branches, each of which serves as a location setting and a base for all kinds of activity. The fascinating thing about a tree is that these locations are related to one another through its special relativity rather than being hermetically isolated. to hop across to another branch, hear one's voice from above, or participate in a conversation happening across branches amongst members of other branches. These are a few of the rich experiences that come from living so densely in space.
