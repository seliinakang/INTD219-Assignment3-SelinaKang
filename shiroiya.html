SHIROIYA HOTEL

Maebashi City, Gunma, Japan

2020

Creative minds from Japan and abroad gathered in Maebashi, Gunma to revitalize the city once prospered in the silk industry. Shiroiya Hotel is a living room for the locals ad travelers to relax and enjoy art, food, and green. It served also as a cultural axis of Maebashi where various projects are in progress for the city to grow. Each room offers a new aesthetic experience for guests, who can avail themselves of sauna rooms, or Gault & Millau-listed local Joshu cuisine in the open-style dining restaurant.
