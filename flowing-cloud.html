SOU FUJIMOTO

FLOWING CLOUD PAVILION

Tonglu County, Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China

2022

The location itself served as inspiration for the design, particularly the clouds and fog that dispersed over the settlement. They resembled buildings suspended in midair, or more accurately, roofs linking surrounding scenery, structures, and the natural world. The environment was incredibly vibrant and bright due to the continuous terraced fields, which also formed an architectural picture. Like a cloud, a floating canopy that embraces and blends with the scenery.
