SOU FUJIMOTO

CANOPIA

Bordeaux, France

2016

This mixed-use development, located in Bordeaux's Gare Saint Jean neighborhood, is made up of residential buildings, offices, a panoramic bar, stores, and shared spaces. It has 500 m² of retail space, 3,770 m² of office space, and 199 residential units. The development, called Canopia, wants to become the center of attention for the city. It is an urban landmark that transforms into a star or a lighthouse on this regional city's skyline at dusk. Both city dwellers and tourists will appreciate the exclusive viewpoint they have over the surroundings.
